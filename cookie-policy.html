Cookie policy.

Last updated: 25 April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how 3R Padel Camp uses cookies and similar technologies on its website at 3rpadelcamp.com. It applies together with our Privacy Policy and is provided in accordance with Article 22.2 of Spanish Law 34/2002 (LSSI-CE) and the guidance issued by the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD).

— 01

What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file that a website stores in your browser when you visit it. Cookies allow a site to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences, so that subsequent visits can work more efficiently and securely. Some cookies are essential for the site to function. Others are optional and are only used with your consent.

For the purposes of this policy, we use the term "cookies" to also refer to similar tracking technologies such as pixel tags, web beacons, scripts, and local storage.

— 02

Our approach

3R Padel Camp's website is a single informational page. We have deliberately kept its technical footprint minimal:

We do not use: Google Analytics, Meta (Facebook) Pixel, advertising cookies, behavioural-advertising tracking, social-media tracking pixels, or any third-party analytics or marketing tools.

The only cookies that may be set when you visit our site are technical cookies set by the providers we use to host and protect the site. These are described in the next section.

— 03

Cookies used on this website

Strictly necessary / technical cookies

These cookies are required for the website to function correctly and securely. Under Article 22.2 LSSI-CE, technical cookies that are strictly necessary do not require prior consent.

Cookie / technology Set by Purpose Duration
__cf_bm Cloudflare (third party) Bot management. Distinguishes genuine human visitors from automated bots, helping protect the site from malicious traffic. ~30 minutes
cf_clearance Cloudflare (third party) Records that a visitor has passed Cloudflare's security challenges, so the same challenge is not repeated on every page. Up to 1 year
Email obfuscation script Cloudflare (third party) Hides email addresses on the page from automated scrapers. Runs locally in your browser; does not normally set persistent cookies. Session only

Other third-party services that may receive your IP address

The following services are loaded by our website but, to the best of our knowledge, do not set cookies in your browser. They may, however, log your IP address as part of normal request handling:

  • Google Fonts (Google LLC) — used to deliver the typefaces you see on this site (Bebas Neue, DM Serif Display, DM Sans). When your browser loads a font, your IP address is sent to Google's servers. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc., a Microsoft subsidiary) — used to host the static files of this website. See GitHub's Privacy Statement.
— 04

Your consent

Because the cookies described in Section 3 are strictly necessary for security and the technical operation of the website, they fall within the exemption in Article 22.2 LSSI-CE and may be set without prior consent.

If, in the future, we add cookies that are not strictly necessary (for example, analytics or marketing cookies), we will update this policy and request your prior, informed, and revocable consent through a cookie consent banner before any such cookies are set.

— 05

International data transfers

Cloudflare, Google, and GitHub are companies based in the United States. Information processed through these services (such as your IP address) may therefore be transferred outside the European Economic Area. These transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards under the GDPR, in particular:

  • The EU–U.S. Data Privacy Framework, where the recipient is certified.
  • The European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Supplementary technical and organisational measures where appropriate.

For more information, please consult the privacy policies of these providers, linked in Section 3.

— 06

How to disable or delete cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies, including the technical cookies described above. Please be aware that doing so may affect the proper functioning of this website and other websites you visit.

Instructions for the most popular browsers are available here:

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Apple Safari — Preferences → Privacy
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions
  • Safari for iOS — Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security
  • Chrome for Android — Settings → Site settings → Cookies

For other browsers, please consult their official documentation.

— 07

Profiling and automated decisions

3R Padel Camp does not create user profiles based on cookies, and does not make automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects on visitors.
