What is 3R Padel Camp?

More than
just training.

3R Padel Camp is an immersive 3-day experience designed for adult players who want to genuinely level up their game. Set on the stunning Costa Blanca coastline, this is where technique meets competition, community, and sun.

Whether you're looking to sharpen your tactics, play real matches against local competition, or simply fall deeper in love with padel — this camp was built for you.

Refine. Rally. Rise.

Every session is crafted to push your limits — from on-court technical drills to beach physical training and a mini-tournament to close it all out.
